PROJECT 4 (COMING SOON)

Once upon a time, in a world that was entirely flat, lived a small, lonely square named Tommy. Everything in his town was square: the trees, the houses, and even the food. Tommy was bored. One day, while digging in the dirt, he found something strange. It was brown, heavy, and completely round. It was a ball. Tommy pushed the ball, and it rolled. He had never seen anything roll before! He laughed and chased it, his corners digging into the dirt as he scrambled after it. The ball bounced off a square tree and landed in a square puddle with a satisfying splosh. "What is this magic?" asked his neighbor, Sarah, who had come over to see what all the fuss was about. "Its a ball!" said Tommy proudly. Word spread quickly about Tommys amazing rolling, bouncing, and squishy object. Soon, all the squares in the town gathered, wanting to play with the ball. They kicked it, threw it, and jumped on it. They invented games, learning to catch and pass, developing their skills and motor skills. The ball transformed their lives from dull, flat routines into a world full of movement and fun. One day, the ball got stuck on top of a very tall square roof. The squares were devastated. "Life isnt worth living without balls!" they cried. Tommy, however, didn't give up. He brought a big ladder and climbed up, retrieving the ball. The town cheered! They realized that the best part wasn't just having the ball, but playing together. They even found different types of balls—some hard, some soft, some that made noise—and had a huge ball party under a giant, twinkling, round disco ball. From that day on, the town of Squareville was filled with laughter and the sound of balls rolling and bouncing, proving that sometimes, all it takes is a little roundness to make life more interesting.
